About

A life devoted
to percussion.

Native of Yokohama, Japan, Chihiro Shibayama is a versatile freelance percussionist and educator now based in Longmont, Colorado. Her career spans Broadway, opera, orchestra, new music, and television — from the pit of Miss Saigon to the stage of Carnegie Hall.

She holds Bachelor's and Master's degrees from The Juilliard School and has spent over two decades performing and teaching at the highest levels of the profession.
