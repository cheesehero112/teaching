Frequently asked

Questions answered.

Do I need my own instrument to start?+
Not necessarily! For beginners, I'll guide you on exactly what to get and when. In-person students have access to instruments in my studio. Online students will need some equipment at home — I'm happy to recommend affordable starter options based on your goals.
What ages do you teach?+
I teach students of all ages — starting around age 6 for beginners — through teens, adults, and returning musicians. My approach is always tailored to each student's goals, experience, and learning style. It's never too late to start.
Do you offer online lessons?+
Yes! Private 1-on-1 lessons are available both in-person (Longmont, CO) and online worldwide. Semi-private kids' lessons are currently in-person only.
What is your cancellation and makeup policy?+
Semester packages include 2 makeup lessons per term. Drop-in lessons must be canceled at least 24 hours in advance to avoid being charged. I understand life happens and always try to be flexible when possible.
What percussion instruments do you teach?+
I teach the full percussion battery — snare drum, marimba, xylophone, vibraphone, timpani, and drum kit fundamentals. Students at the pre-college level can expect to develop proficiency across all major instruments in the classical percussion repertoire.
